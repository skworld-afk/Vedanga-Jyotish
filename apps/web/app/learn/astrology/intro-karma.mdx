<div className="flex flex-col items-center text-center">
  <h2 className="text-3xl sm:text-4xl font-light tracking-tight text-slate-100">
    Karma and <span className="font-semibold text-amber-400">Destiny</span>
  </h2>
  <div className="h-[1px] w-24 bg-amber-500/50 mt-6 mb-4" />
</div>

<div className="text-slate-300 leading-loose text-base mb-4">
  Jyotiṣa studies human destiny not as mere fortune-telling, but as the profound Vedic science revealing when and how the fruits of our past actions ripen. The core definition of Jyotiṣa is <strong className="text-amber-400">"Karmaphala-vipāka-kāla-vidhānam"</strong> — the knowledge of when karmic fruits will mature.
</div>

<div className="text-slate-300 leading-loose text-base mb-4">
  All accumulated results across lifetimes reside in the <strong className="text-purple-400">Karmāśaya</strong> (repository of karma), divided into <strong className="text-rose-400">Sañcita</strong> (stored) and <strong className="text-emerald-400">Kriyamāṇa</strong> or <strong className="text-emerald-400">Prārabdha</strong> (active). Karmas of a similar nature cluster together to dictate the <strong className="text-cyan-400">Yoni</strong> (type of existence). The strongest cluster triggers the next birth, transitioning into <em className="text-emerald-400 not-italic font-semibold">Kriyamāṇa</em> — the active karma shaping the current life.
</div>

<div className="text-slate-300 leading-loose text-base mb-4">
  Your <strong className="text-sky-400">Janma Kuṇḍalī</strong> (birth chart) exclusively maps your <em className="text-emerald-400 not-italic font-semibold">Kriyamāṇa Karmāśaya</em> (ripe karma for this life), while the rest slumbers in <em className="text-rose-400 not-italic font-semibold">Sañcita</em>. New actions join the <em className="text-rose-400 not-italic font-semibold">Sañcita</em> until potent enough to become <em className="text-emerald-400 not-italic font-semibold">Kriyamāṇa</em>. Because a <span className="text-indigo-400 font-semibold">Jīva</span> occupies only one <em className="text-cyan-400 not-italic font-semibold">Yoni</em> at a time, only <em className="text-rose-400 not-italic font-semibold">Sañcita</em> karma aligning with the current <em className="text-cyan-400 not-italic font-semibold">Yoni</em> can fructify (<strong className="text-teal-400">Kriyamāṇa Vipāka</strong>), while the rest awaits future births (<strong className="text-pink-400">Sañcita Vipāka</strong>). 
</div>

<div className="text-slate-300 leading-loose text-base mb-4">
  This explains why some effortlessly reap their <em className="text-emerald-400 not-italic font-semibold">Kriyamāṇa Prārabdha</em>, whereas others' efforts remain pending in the <em className="text-rose-400 not-italic font-semibold">Sañcita</em> stream. According to <span className="text-amber-400 font-semibold">Maharṣi Parāśara</span>, a horoscope maps this active portion of destiny by reading the planetary patterns at the exact moment of birth.
</div>
